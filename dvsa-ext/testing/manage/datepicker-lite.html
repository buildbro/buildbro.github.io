What tests are not included in the calendar?

Not all tests are shown in the calendar for the following reasons:

  • if you've entered your instructor's reference number, you won't see any times when they're unavailable
  • cancellations are always shown - but there may be none available
  • any appointments that are after the expiry date of your theory test will not be shown
